Anime
Adventure

Synopsis

Mashle: Magic and Muscles is a comedy-action anime set in a world where magic is everything. The story follows Mash Burnedead, a boy born without magic in a society that worships it. Instead of magic, Mash relies on his insane physical strength, using pure muscle power to overcome powerful wizards and magical challenges. He enrolls in a magic academy and defeats enemies in the most unexpected (and hilarious) ways.
