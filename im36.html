Kaiju No. 8: Mission Recon (2018)

Synopsis

Kaiju No. 8: Mission Recon is an action-packed sci-fi movie set in a world threatened by giant monsters called Kaiju. It follows Kafka Hibino, a man who gains the power to transform into a Kaiju while fighting alongside an elite defense force. The film blends intense battles, dark humor, and themes of duty, identity, and chasing dreams despite the odds.
