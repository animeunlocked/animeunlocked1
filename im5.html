Synopsis

One Punch Man is a superhero action-comedy anime about Saitama, a man so powerful that he defeats any enemy with just one punch. While facing monsters and villains effortlessly, he struggles with boredom and the desire for true challenge. The series blends intense action, humor, and satire of typical hero stories.
