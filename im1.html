Jujutsu Kaisen


Jujutsu Kaisen is a dark fantasy anime set in a world where cursed spirits are born from negative human emotions. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes involved in jujutsu sorcery after swallowing a powerful cursed object. Trained by elite sorcerers, he fights deadly curses while struggling with the dangerous power inside him. The anime is known for its intense action, deep characters, and stunning animation.
